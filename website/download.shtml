Installation:
    Mount the image file and copy iTerm.app to anywhere you want. If you have an older version, amke sure it's either over-written or completely gone (i.e. not in trash). OS X sometimes has weird behavior about that.
Notice:
    Due a change of bookmark organizations introduced in 0.8.0, old bookmark settings are no longer recognized. You have to re-enter these bookmarks manually into the new version.
    Version 0.8.0 comes with a couple of predefined keymapping/display/terminal profiles. If you do not see them, please delete your iTerm.plist file in ~/Library/Preferences and restart the program.
